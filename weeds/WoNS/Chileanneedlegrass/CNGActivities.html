Regional Activities and Events

Register an upcoming event that will assist Chilean needle grass land managers. Demonstration sites are frequently established by Landcare, extension and research organisations. This site provides a way to invite others to visit your demonstration site or discuss your research findings.

Upcoming events:
Use the feedback page list to register an upcoming event. Please include what the event is, date, time, location, topics to be addressed, cost and contact information.

Demonstration sites:
Use the feedback page list to register a demonstration site. Please include the demonstration topic, date established, location, specific aspects addressed, and contact information.
