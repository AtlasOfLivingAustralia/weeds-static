Identification

    Plant identification resource

    Bellyache bush can be confused with two other introduced plants - physic nut and castor oil plant. It is important to correctly identify any potential weed before considering appropriate control measures. These two tools will assist you to make the correct identification for bellyache bush.

    National Management Map         		Bellyache Bush Catchment Management Objectives
    June 2013
    Objectives have been assigned to each catchment in Australia for the management of bellyache bush. The map can be used as a guide for state / territory, regional and community groups in determining priority actions, projects and funding submissions.
    The definition of each of the five management objectives, together with the criteria used to assign those objectives is available here.
