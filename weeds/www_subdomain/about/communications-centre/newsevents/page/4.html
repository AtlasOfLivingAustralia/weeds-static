News from the Geospatial Team

3 March, 2010

Three programmers have just been appointed to the geospatial team to help develop the Spatial Analysis Toolkit. A workshop was held in Canberra in early December 2009 to provide advice to the Atlas on the toolkit. A report of the workshop is now available.
More News from the Geospatial Team

Jörg and the travelling BioCASe circus

3 March, 2010

BioCASe is the web service that can deliver data associated with plant specimens from herbaria. The Atlas of Living Australia has sponsored Jörg Holetschek, a BioCASe specialist who works at the Botanischer Garten und Botanisches Museum Berlin-Dahlem, to come to Australia and build local expertise.
More Jörg and the travelling BioCASe circus

DataOne: Research data assessment

2 March, 2010

The Data Observation Network for Earth (DataONE) is inviting scientists to participate in a US National Science Foundation (NSF) sponsored research study about scientists and how they work. The results of this study will help them create better tools to support scientific discovery.
