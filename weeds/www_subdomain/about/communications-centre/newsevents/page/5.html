TDWG conference in Fremantle, October 2008

10 November, 2008

Taxonomic Database Working Group (TDWG) held its annual conference in Fremantle, WA, 19-26 October 2008. This was the largest TDWG meeting ever, with around 180 participants from all continents, including 60 from across Australia. Links are provided to the agenda, abstracts and presentations.

Bioinformatician positions advertised

TDWG 2008 conference to be held in Perth

14 June, 2008

TDWG the international biodiversity information standards organisation, will hold its annual conference at the Western Australian Maritime Museum, Perth, 19-25 October 2008. A full day symposium on the Atlas of Living Australia will take place on Wednesday 22 October.
