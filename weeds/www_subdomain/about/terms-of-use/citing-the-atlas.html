Citing the Atlas

Citing the Atlas

To cite the Atlas of Living Australia (Atlas), follow these guidelines.

  • To cite the Atlas as a whole, use:

‘Atlas of Living Australia website at http://www.ala.org.au. Accessed 29 October 2010.’

  • To cite a page from the Atlas, use the form

‘Atlas of Living Australia website at http://www.ala.org.au/page name. Accessed 3 November 2010.’

Example: ‘Atlas of Living Australia website at http://bie.ala.org.au/species/Vombatus+ursinus. Accessed 30 November 2011.’

Note: As with most websites, pages on the Atlas website may change over time.
