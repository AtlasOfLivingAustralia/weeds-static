Council of Heads of Australian Faunal Collections

The Council of Heads of Australian Faunal Collections (CHAFC) represents the major Australian zoological collections and is making data from the Online Zoological Collections of Australian Museums (OZCAM) available through the Atlas of Living Australia.

Contribution to the ALA

OZCAM is an online distributed network of databases that contain information about the faunal (animal) collections held in Australian museums and other institutions, such as CSIRO.
