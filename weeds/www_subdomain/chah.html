Council of Heads of Australasian Herbaria

The Council of Heads of Australasian Herbaria (CHAH, http://www.anbg.gov.au/chah/) represents the major Australasian botanical collections and is making data from Australia's Virtual Herbarium (AVH) through the Atlas of Living Australia.

Contribution to the ALA

AVH is an on-line botanical information resource providing access to the wealth of data associated with scientific plant specimens in each Australian herbarium, including six million specimen records, images, descriptive text and identification tools.

The AVH is a collaborative project of the State, Commonwealth and Territory herbaria, developed under the auspices of (CHAH).
