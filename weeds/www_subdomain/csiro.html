CSIRO

CSIRO (http://www.csiro.au/) is acting as the host organisation for the Atlas of Living Australia and employs a small team of ALA staff based at CSIRO Entomology in Canberra.

Contribution to the ALA

CSIRO is contributing data from its collections:

  • Australian National Herbarium (ANH)
  • Australian National Insect Collection (ANIC)
  • Australian National Fish Collection (ANFC)
  • Australian National Wildlife Collection (ANWC)

During 2007-2008 the CSIRO collections will be developing a range of content for the Atlas, including:

  • Species information on fishes as part of the Fishes of Australia Online project
  • Images of a large number of Australian fish species
  • Images of Australian insect species, including representative images for each Australian insect family
  • Molecular sequences and specimen data for Australian termites
  • Molecular sequences and specimen data for a range of vertebrate species
  • Images and identification tools for Australian reptiles and amphibians
  • Species information, images and specimen data for Australian mangrove plant species
  • Nomenclatural data for Australian plant species as part of the Australian Plant Names Index
  • Information on the state distributions for Australian plant species
  • Images of Australian plants and of original descriptions of Australian plants
  • Nomenclatural data on Australian hornworts and liverworts
