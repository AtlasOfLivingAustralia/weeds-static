Practical Responses to Climate Change Conference

28 November, 2011

The second “Practical responses to climate change” conference will be held in Canberra from 1-3 May 2012.

The theme for 2012 is “Water and climate: policy implementation challenges”. This conference will be an opportunity for policy makers, engineers, scientists, planners and academics to present, hear and debate the latest research and practice on water and climate policy implementation challenges in urban, catchment and coastal environments.

Papers on a range of themes are invited for oral or poster presentation. All papers will be peer reviewed and accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings.

Visit the conference website at www.climatechange2012.org for further information regarding conference themes, and to upload your abstract.

Important dates

  • Abstract Submission Deadline: Monday 31 October 2011
  • Abstract Acceptance Notification: Friday 11 November 2011
  • Full Paper Submission Deadline: Monday 6 February 2012
