1.   To use the Zoom and Pan to Area function, hold down the shift key and drag the mouse button and drag the mouse across the map to select the area that you want to zoom in to.

2.   To go back to the default map view, use the sizing navigation icon.

Figure 4: Map Sizing Navigation Icons

3.   The Map sizing navigation icon allows you to adjust the scale of the map.  In this case, left click and drag the slider towards minus.
